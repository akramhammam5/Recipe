Skillet Chicken Pasta

Things To Consider:

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time: 25 mins

Ingredients

  1. ½ (8 ounce) package spaghettini
  2. tablespoons olive oil, divided
  3. 8 roma (plum) tomatoes, halved and sliced
  4. 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  5. ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  6. 2 teaspoons dried basil
  7. 1 pinch salt
  8. 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  9. 1 ½ teaspoons white sugar
  10. 1 tablespoon ketchup
  11. 2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
  12. 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  13. 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  14. 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  15. 1 red onion, chopped
  16. 1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
  17. ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
